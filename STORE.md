## Full description (paste into Play Console)
Space Boosters is a fast, single-player capture-the-flag arcade game
built for one-thumb play.

You pilot a small ship — boost to escape danger or fire projectiles
in shooter mode. Tag the enemy carrier, grab their flag, and run it
home before they get yours.

Features
• 5 hand-built maps with different layouts
• 3 bot difficulties — Easy, Medium, Hard
• Two play modes — Classic (boost) and Shooter (projectiles)
• Story mode (first to N points wins)
• Freeplay mode with adjustable team sizes (1–4 bots per team)
• Smart bot teammates with offensive / defensive / auto roles
• Per-bot difficulty tuning in freeplay
• Local match progress — beat each level on Hard

No ads. No accounts. No internet required.
