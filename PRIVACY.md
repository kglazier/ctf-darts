# Privacy Policy — Space Boosters

**Effective date:** 2026-04-25

Space Boosters (the "App") does not collect, store, or transmit any
personal information about you.

## What the app does

The App has two play modes:

- **Solo:** runs entirely offline. No internet connection is used.
- **Online:** lets you play with friends in the same lobby. Online mode
  uses the internet — see "Online play" below.

## What the app stores

The App stores game progress on your device — specifically, the highest
difficulty level you have beaten on each map. This information is saved
in a local file (`progress.json`) on your device and is never sent
anywhere.

## Online play

When you choose **Play Online** and host or join a lobby, the App:

- Connects to a small "signaling" server only long enough to find the
  other player(s) in your lobby. The signaling server sees your lobby
  code and the technical information needed to set up a peer-to-peer
  connection (IP/port hints, called "ICE candidates"). It does not see
  your gameplay, your name, or any account information — the App has no
  account system.
- After connection, all gameplay traffic flows **directly between the
  players' devices** (peer-to-peer over WebRTC). It does not pass through
  any server. We do not log, store, or have access to this traffic.
- Your device's IP address is necessarily visible to the other players
  in your lobby (this is how peer-to-peer works) and to the signaling
  server while you are connecting. Don't host games with strangers if
  you are uncomfortable with this.

The App does not store any record of past lobbies, players you have
played with, or game results.

## What the app does not do

- The App does not contain advertisements.
- The App does not include third-party analytics or tracking.
- The App does not access your contacts, photos, location, microphone,
  camera, or any other personal data.
- The App has no user accounts, login, or registration.
- Solo mode does not make any network requests.

## Children's privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children. If
your child uses online mode, be aware that their device's IP address is
visible to other players in their lobby (see "Online play" above).
